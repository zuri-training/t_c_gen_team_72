What is personal Information?

Personal information is information that is linked or reasonably linked,
directly or indirectly, to an identified or identifiable natural person.
Personal information can include:

  • Personal identifiers (e.g., names, addresses, emails, ID numbers)
  • Photos, sound recordings of voices
  • Internet activity(e.g., internet browsing history, search history, page
    views)
  • Online identifiers (e.g., IP address, cookie identifiers
  • Geolocation information (can be derived from IP address)
  • Inferences from other personal information

What does "collect" mean?

Collecting information means you
gather details about your users
either directly (e.g., via email,
website form, sign-up page, survey) or from other sources
(e.g., pubicly available information, data scraping,
marketing lists, analytics
software)
