What is a privacy policy?

A privacy policy (also referred to as a privacy notice) is a statement or legal document that dexcribes how a company,
website, or app collects, maintains, and shares information collected from or about its users. Most Countries
require any website that collects personal information from its users to have a written privacy policy posted on the website.
